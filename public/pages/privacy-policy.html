Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

1. Introduction

WebDrop is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our peer-to-peer file sharing service.

2. Information We Don't Collect

WebDrop is designed with privacy at its core. We do not:

  • Store any files transferred through our service
  • Track user activities or behavior
  • Collect personal information
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Share any data with third parties

3. Technical Data

The only technical data temporarily processed includes:

  • Temporary peer IDs for establishing connections
  • WebRTC connection data for peer-to-peer transfers

This data is automatically deleted when you close your browser tab or end the session.

4. GDPR Compliance

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we ensure:

  • No personal data is collected or stored
  • No data processing activities require consent
  • No data is transferred to third countries
  • No automated decision-making or profiling

5. Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all file transfers
  • Direct peer-to-peer connections
  • No server-side storage of files or data
  • Secure WebRTC protocols

6. Children's Privacy

WebDrop does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As we don't collect personal information, no special measures are required under COPPA.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of any material changes through our website.

8. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
